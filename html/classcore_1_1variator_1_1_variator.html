Appy the variator to a tuple of parents Produce a tuple of genomes from a tuple of genomes.

The input and output tuple sizes should match the arity and coarity of this selector, respectively. Implementation note: clear the score first.

Reimplemented in core.main.RandomBitMutator, evolvables.expression.ProgramCrossoverVariator, and core.variator.DefaultVariator.
